Reclaimed Creations

At Reclaimed Creations, we breathe new life into used bullet shells, crafting them into functional, beautiful products. From bottle openers to jewelry, each piece is thoughtfully designed to be a conversation starter and a symbol of sustainability.

Explore our range of products made with care, and join us in supporting a circular economy where nothing goes to waste. Each item tells a story and embodies the transformation from impact to inspiration.
